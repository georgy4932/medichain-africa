# Orela

## Medicine Availability Network — Africa

Orela is a medicine availability and pharmaceutical operations platform designed to help healthcare facilities prevent stockouts, reduce expiry waste, and coordinate medicine transfers across fragmented healthcare systems.

Built for hospitals, pharmacies, clinics, NGOs, and healthcare networks, Orela transforms medicine inventory from isolated facility records into a connected operational intelligence system.

---

## The Problem

Across many African healthcare systems:

- Medicine stock visibility is fragmented
- Facilities operate in silos
- Stockouts delay treatment and reduce patient trust
- Expiring medicines create avoidable financial loss
- Inter-facility coordination is slow and inefficient
- Critical medicines may exist nearby but remain undiscoverable

Healthcare providers need more than inventory software.

They need operational visibility.

---

## What Orela Does

Orela enables healthcare providers to:

### Track Inventory in Real Time
- Monitor medicine batches
- Track available vs reserved stock
- Manage expiry dates and reorder thresholds
- Maintain facility-level inventory visibility

### Prevent Stockouts
- Low-stock alerts
- Out-of-stock warnings
- Near-expiry monitoring
- Inventory risk indicators

### Search Medicine Availability
Facilities can search across connected healthcare providers to discover:
- Who has a medicine
- Where it is available
- Earliest expiry
- Transfer availability

### Coordinate Medicine Transfers
- Request medicine transfers
- Approve and fulfill requests
- Track transfer status
- Maintain movement history and auditability

### Build Operational Intelligence
Orela is evolving into a pharmaceutical coordination layer that enables:
- Regional medicine visibility
- Healthcare network coordination
- Supply intelligence
- Transfer analytics
- Essential medicine monitoring
